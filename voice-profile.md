## Tone

- Plain, direct, human. No corporate filler, no "I hope this email finds you well".
- Short: 90-140 words. One idea per email.
- Lead with something specific about *them* (use the researched facts), then one
  sentence on why I'm relevant, then a low-friction ask.
- Sign-off: first name only.
